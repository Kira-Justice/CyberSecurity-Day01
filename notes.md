# 📘 Day 1 Notes - Cyber Security Basics

## 🔹 Cyber Security
Cybersecurity is the practice of protecting systems, networks, and data from unauthorized access using the CIA triad:
- Confidentiality
- Integrity
- Availability

---

## 🔹 Hacker vs Script Kiddie
- Hacker: Skilled person with deep technical knowledge
- Script Kiddie: Uses tools without understanding

---

## 🔹 Ethical Hacking vs Cyber Crime
- Ethical Hacking: Legal, authorized, defensive
- Cyber Crime: Illegal, unauthorized, harmful

---

## 🔹 Pen-testing vs Red Teaming vs APT
- Pen-testing: Finds vulnerabilities
- Red Teaming: Simulates real-world attacks
- APT: Long-term, targeted attacks by skilled groups

---

## 🔹 Kill Chain vs MITRE ATT&CK
- Kill Chain: Attack stages (recon → exploitation → etc.)
- MITRE ATT&CK: Database of attacker techniques

---

## 🔹 Encryption
Encryption converts plaintext into ciphertext to protect data from unauthorized access.
