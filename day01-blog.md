# 🧠 Day 1 Reflection - My Cybersecurity Journey

Today was my first structured step into cybersecurity, and it completely changed how I think about hacking and security.

## 🔍 What I Learned
I learned that hacking is not just about tools—it’s about mindset, understanding systems, and thinking deeply. The difference between a hacker and a script kiddie showed me the importance of knowledge over tools.

I also understood the importance of ethical hacking and how professionals legally test systems to improve security.

## 🧪 Practical Experience
Using CyberChef, I performed encoding and encryption. This helped me understand how data is transformed and protected.

## ⚠️ Challenges
Some concepts like MITRE ATT&CK and Red Teaming are still new to me, and I need to explore them deeper.

## 🎯 My Goal
I want to become a skilled penetration tester and understand how real-world attacks work so I can defend against them.

## 💬 Final Thought
"Real hacking is not crime — it’s discipline, ethics, and deep technical skill."
